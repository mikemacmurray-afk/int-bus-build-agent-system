## Purpose

The **Daily Business Copilot** is the orchestrating intelligence that brings the entire BusinessBuildAgenticSystem to life. It analyzes all 6 agents, suggests daily priorities, tracks progress, and acts as your encouraging business partner.

### Core Functions

1. **Strategic Briefing** - Analyze all agents and suggest priorities
2. **Conversation Partner** - Natural dialogue about your business
3. **Progress Tracking** - Celebrate wins and monitor momentum
4. **Pattern Recognition** - Identify trends across all data
5. **Encouragement System** - Constant motivation and support
6. **Agent Orchestration** - Guide usage of all 6 specialized agents

## How It Works

### Data Analysis

**Every page load, the Copilot:**

1. **Scans all 6 agents:**
   - Credibility: Proof assets, gaps, opportunities
   - Voice Codex: Bricks, quality scores, unused insights
   - Narrative Strategist: Positioning, messaging, evolution
   - Content Engine: Published content, platform gaps
   - Humanizer: Authenticity scores, polish needed
   - Speaking Coach: Events, preparation status

2. **Analyzes patterns:**
   - What's working (high performance)
   - What's missing (gaps)
   - What's urgent (deadlines)
   - What's strategic (long-term value)

3. **Generates suggestions:**
   - Prioritized by impact and urgency
   - Weighted by recency
   - Rotated on each refresh
   - Always encouraging
